Veggie Pizza

How to make a Veggie Pizza

This is our ultimate oven-baked veggie lover’s pizza. Red sauce, mozzarella, red onions, olives, bell peppers, artichokes, and a golden crust. It’s all here!

Ingredients

  • 2 (1-pound) store-bought or homemade pizza dough
  • Mushrooms
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Asparagus
  • Olives
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450ºF
  2. Divide and shape the dough into balls
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet
  5. Change the oven from baking to broiling
  6. Add the toppings
  7. Bake the pizza
  8. Slice and serve!
